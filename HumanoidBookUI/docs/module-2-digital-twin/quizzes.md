# Quizzes: Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

## Quiz 1: Digital Twin Fundamentals

### 1. What is the primary purpose of a digital twin in robotics?

- a) To replace physical robots entirely  
- b) To provide a virtual replica for simulation, testing, and validation  
- c) To create video games featuring robots  
- d) To store robot design files  

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer</strong></summary>

**Correct Answer:** b) To provide a virtual replica for simulation, testing, and validation

</details>

### 2. Which of the following best describes the reality gap in simulation?

## Quiz 4: Simulation-to-Reality Transfer

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer</strong></summary>

**Correct Answer:** b) Domain randomization

</details>
